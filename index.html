About DeepSim

We're a passionate indie game studio driven by the desire to create multiplayer simulation experiences that spark imagination. By blending handcrafted art, intelligent backend systems, and the versatility of Unity, we're building games that are not only deeply engaging but also feel alive and constantly changing.

Our goal is simple: to create living, breathing worlds that you can explore and shape with your friends, forging unique stories and unforgettable moments.
